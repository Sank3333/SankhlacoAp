About Us

M.S. Sankhla & Co. was established in the year 2002. In the year 2011 Sarshan Corporate Services Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated as business entity. Subsequently in the year 2018 the entity was changed to Sarshan HR Solutions India Private Limited with the aim to outsource labour law consultancy services to corporate clients and to render labour and Industrial law including end to end compliance’s services.

We are present in the domain of Payroll Management and labour law compliance’s for the past many years. We offer end-to-end solutions including labour law consultancy, payroll processing, ESIC/EPF management, compliance audits, and litigation support. With a strong pan-India presence, we cater to clients across diverse sectors such as manufacturing, IT, FMCG, pharma, and logistics.
